Foundations

Two grounds, one accent

The app is theme-aware end to end: a .dark class on the root swaps a handful of CSS custom properties, and every inline color: var(--navy) plus each dark: utility flips together. Navy is the structural voice, blue the single interactive accent, gold reserved for highlight and warning.

Aa
System sans — ui-sans-serif / system-ui. The app ships no webfont; hierarchy comes from weight, tracking & the navy colour-fade.
Navy · Blue · Gold — structure, action, accent. Semantic status hues stay separate from the accent.
Elevation — soft, wide, low-opacity shadows; 12–16px radii. Cards feel lifted, not boxed.
